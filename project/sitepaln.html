Site Plan for: HydroGel Pro

Description: "HydroGel Pro" represents a professional service for applying high-quality hydrogel screen protectors on smartphones and tablets using the automated M288 machine, ensuring precision and speed.

Site Purpose

The site provides information about our hydrogel application services, pricing, and the benefits of using automated, precise installation. Customers can view service options, book appointments, and contact the team for inquiries.

Scenarios / Visitor Questions

  • Scenario 1: How much does it cost to apply a hydrogel screen protector on my smartphone?
  • Scenario 2: Can I schedule an appointment online and choose a time slot?

Color Schema

  • Main Color (headings, borders, accents): #1a73e8 (blue – trust & technology)
  • Secondary Color (highlights, links, buttons): #e91e63 (pink – accent & call-to-action)

Typography

  • Headings: Roboto, sans-serif (modern and clean)
  • Body: Arial, sans-serif (easy to read)
